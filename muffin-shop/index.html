About Us

The Muffin Shop brings taste delights for all - vegan, vegetarian, paleo, keto and everything else. Master bakers with over 25 years of experience offer tasty, delicious and sometimes even healthy muffins for your pleasure. Our daily selection of hand-crafted muffins includes unique seasonal creations to the classics. Enjoy with a friend, share with co-workers or take home to the family. Our bakery offers muffins that are fresh baked every day. Breakfast, lunch, desert - there is a muffin that is just right! Catering services offered, gift certificates available. We are suitable for every occasion.

Catering

Since 2010 The Muffin Shop has provided top-class catering. When you need lots of coffe and a few hundred muffins, we are the ones you want to work with! Reliable and consistent. Fresh gourmet coffee and muffins are served from our signature muffin trolley or cart. We have an experienced uniformed catering crew providing professional service for events ranging from 50 to 300. We will provide the plates, cups, napkins, plastic ware and condiments. Ask us about our menu of muffins which can be customized for your wedding or private event. We also can provide an array of great specialty teas and coffees. Impress your friends and co-workers with a Muffin Shop catered event!
